About Our Lab

Advanced Computing Environment

The Computer Lab at NIT Patna is a hub of technological innovation and academic excellence. Our lab provides students with access to cutting-edge hardware and software to facilitate their learning and research activities.

With a team of experienced technicians and faculty members, we ensure that our students have the best possible environment to develop their technical skills and work on innovative projects.
